*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Knights_of_the_Old_Republic_Campaign_Guide)*

An insulating glove with an embedded [Energy Cell](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Energy_Cell), the Stunning Gauntlet produces an electrifying charge when it strikes a target. A Stunning Gauntlet changes the wearer's [Unarmed Attacks](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Unarmed_Attacks) to [Stun](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Stun) damage, and they provide a +1 bonus to [Stun](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Stun) damage on a successful [Unarmed Attack](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Unarmed_Attack).

A Stunning Gauntlet is two sizes smaller than their wearer (for example, a Stunning Gauntlet designed for a [[03_Regole/03.02_Specie/human.md|Human]] are Tiny). Because of how they are worn, a Stunning Gauntlet can't be [Disarmed](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Disarmed) or dropped.
