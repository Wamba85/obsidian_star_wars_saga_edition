*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Jedi Academy Training Manual](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Jedi_Academy_Training_Manual)*

Favored by Large [Species](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Species), the Great Lightsaber produces an oversized Lightsaber blade. The blade of a Great Lightsaber is much longer and thicker than that of a normal [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber_(Weapon)), and the handle itself is nearly as long as that of a [[04_Equipaggiamento/04.01_Armi/double-bladed-lightsaber.md|Double-Bladed Lightsaber]].

Only Large (or larger) creatures can use the Great Lightsaber in conjunction with [Feats](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Feats) or [Talents](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Talents) that affect Light Weapons or [Lightsabers](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsabers) (such as the [Weapon Finesse](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Finesse) feat or [Talents](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Talents) from the [[03_Regole/03.04_Talenti/melee-duelist-talent-tree.md|Melee Duelist Talent Tree]]).

A Great Lightsaber requires a special [Energy Cell](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Energy_Cell) to operate (the cost is ten times the price of an ordinary [Energy Cell](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Energy_Cell), but it lasts almost indefinitely).
