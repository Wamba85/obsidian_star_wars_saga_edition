**Weapon Type:** [Simple Weapons (Melee)](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Simple_Weapons_(Melee))

**Size:** Tiny

**Cost:** 25

**Damage:** 1d4

**Weight:** 1 Kilogram

**Type:** Slashing or Piercing

**Special:** Can be [Thrown](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown)
