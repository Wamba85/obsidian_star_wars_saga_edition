*Homebrew Reference Book: [Corporate Sector Sourcebook](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Corporate_Sector_Sourcebook)*

A premier atmospheric fighter, the K-222 Aero-Interceptor is one of the fastest fighters in its class. The Aero-Interceptor features a [Laser Cannon](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Laser_Cannon) and [Concussion Missiles](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Concussion_Missiles), as well as powerful ion engines.
