*Reference Book: Star Wars Saga Edition Core Rulebook*

The LAAT/i Attack Gunship, first deployed at the Battle of [Geonosis](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Geonosis), is an example of a combat space transport designed to carry combat troops directly into the heat of battle. Its weapons are used to clear a landing zone and lay down suppressing fire as the troops exit the [Vehicle](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Vehicle).
