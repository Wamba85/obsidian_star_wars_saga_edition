*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Galaxy of Intrigue](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Galaxy_of_Intrigue)*

The Incom A-24 Sleuth is a highly maneuverable Scout Ship with excellent sublight speed. Despite being older than more popular scout ship models, the sleuth became Mol Hedron's choice for spying on the fledgling New Republic. Most of the time, [The New Republic](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_New_Republic) never even knew he was there. On the rare occasion when they did discover his presence, he was able to complete transmission and jump away.

The modified Sleuth Scout Ship contains a high-powered transmitter capable of sending a nearly undetectable signal by tight beam. Intercepting these signals is extremely difficult. Even if intercepted, the signal is often coded and requires deciphering.
