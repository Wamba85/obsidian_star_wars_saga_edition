Transit*-Class Cargo Shuttle Starship data created by Sterling Hershey ([source](https://www.sterlinghershey.com/blog/2010/9/29/star-wars-wednesday-stat-pack-maps-of-mastery-i.html)).*

The *Transit*-Class Cargo Shuttle is a small craft used by independent pilots to fly small shipments within small sectors of space. The small cargo capacity makes the *Transit*-Class an unappealing target to pirates even without considering the remarkable firepower the Cargo Shuttle can bring to bear.
