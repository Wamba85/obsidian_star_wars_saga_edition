A more effective variant of the [[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/kihraxz-assault-fighter.md|Kihraxz Assault Fighter]], the Vaksai Fighter is a unique modification utilized by [Black Sun](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Sun) during the Galactic Civil War.

These modifications include a more streamlined outer shell, improved energy output for external shielding and firepower, and powerful sublight thrusters to enable more advanced maneuvers by its pilots.
