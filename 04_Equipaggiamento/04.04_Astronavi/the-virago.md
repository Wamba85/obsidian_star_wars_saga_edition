*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Starships of the Galaxy](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Starships_of_the_Galaxy)*

The *Virago* was the personal Starship of [Prince Xizor](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Prince_Xizor), Black Sun's Underlord. He personally commissioned MandalMotors to design a unique [Starfighter](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Starfighter) for his personal use. Xizor's demands were steep; he required a ship that could match the [[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/tie-interceptor.md|TIE Interceptor]] in speed and that was both versatile and combat-capable. The spacecraft that was produced as a result was the first *[[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/starviper-class-assault-fighter.md|StarViper]]*[[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/starviper-class-assault-fighter.md|-Class Assault Fighter]], and the criminal prince christened his ship The *Virago*. To ensure that his ship remained unique and unrivaled, Xizor purchased all rights to the new design. 

The *Viago* served Xizor well for many years, paying for itself many times over. It was eventually lost over [Coruscant](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Coruscant) when Xizor's skyhook was destroyed by the Imperial Super Star Destroyer *Executor*. MandalMotors wasted little time in regaining the rights to the *StarViper* design following Xizor's death. The newest iteration of the StarViper-Class Assault Fighter is not as fast or as agile as Xizor's original prototype, but MandalMotors felt that some concessions were unavoidable in order to bring the price into line with the market as a whole.
