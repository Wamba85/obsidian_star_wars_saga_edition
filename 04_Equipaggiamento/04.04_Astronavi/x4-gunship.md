*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Rebellion Era Campaign Guide](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Rebellion_Era_Campaign_Guide)*

The powerful X4 Gunship is one of Incom's largest vessels to date. Roughly the size of a light freighter, the X4 has two full decks to accomodate a large crew, a limited cargo capacity, and ample access to the ship's [Weapon Systems](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Systems). Six laser turrets and a pair of forward-facing cannons allow the X4 to excel at picket and [Starfighter](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Starfighter) screening roles, as well as at taking on smaller [Capital Ships](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Capital_Ships). Its shielding and defenses are also impressive.

The Rebellion uses the X4 for escort duties, but given its performance as a light assault ship, the Alliance is quickly expanding the gunship's role. Incom's limited manufacturing facilities for a ship of this size make it available in limited numbers. Most of the ships manufactured end up in Rebel hands.
