The BSX-5 Dreadnought is a modified version of the legendary [[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/harrower-class-dreadnought.md|*Harrower*-Class Dreadnought]]. The most notable BSX-5 Dreadnought served as the personal flagship of Sith Lord and Dark Council member Darth Jadus which was named The *Dominator*.
