*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Threats of the Galaxy](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Threats_of_the_Galaxy)*

The VCX-350 is a Corellian Engineering Corporation [Space Transport](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Space_Transport) often used by smugglers after the fall of [The Galactic Empire](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Galactic_Empire). Designed for long-range travel and boasting the newest technology, the VCX-350 Light Freighter can be found in almost any smuggler fleet, including [Talon Karrde's](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Talon_Karrde).
