*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Rebellion Era Campaign Guide](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Rebellion_Era_Campaign_Guide)*

Designed as a light bomber, Sienar Fleet Systems' TIE Oppressor proves unpopular with its test pilots: its additional armaments and reduced solar panel stabilization make it somewhat less maneuverable.

However, it quickly becomes a favorite of elite pilots, who find the additional Missile Launcher provides a handy extra punch. As a result, almost no TIE Oppressor sees action without a veteran pilot at the controls.
