The Y-Wing "Courier" is another example of what happens when technicians have lots of ships of one type, but are told to produce ships of another.

By gutting the [Ion Cannons](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Ion_Cannons), the "Courier" added room for several passengers, extra fuel and supplies, and a backup [Hyperdrive](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Hyperdrive). The main cockpit was also modified to have its canopy split and fold open, allowing all onboard to get on or off simultaneously. The cockpit was also designed to eject from the rest of the Starfighter to serve as an [[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/escape-pod.md|Escape Pod]] (No [Acrobatics](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Acrobatics) check required to enter).
