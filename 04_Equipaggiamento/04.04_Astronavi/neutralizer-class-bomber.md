During [The Sith-Imperial War](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sith-Imperial_War), Sienar Fleet Systems' engineers take the designs for the [[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/predator-class-starfighter.md|*Predator*-Class Starfighter]] and begin working on ways to adapt the design to a multipurpose bomber. The result, dubbed the *Neutralizer*-Class Bomber, is clearly inspired by the [[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/predator-class-starfighter.md|*Predator*-Class Starfighter]] but is larger, bulkier, and somewhat less agile. Still quite nimble for a bomber, the *Neutralizer*-Class Bomber is also very durable, boasting impressive shields for a fighter of its size.

Compared to the [[04_Equipaggiamento/04.04_Astronavi/predator-class-starfighter.md|*Predator*-Class Starfighter]], only a small number of *Neutralizer*-Class Bombers have rolled off assembly lines. Since the end of [The Sith-Imperial War](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Sith-Imperial_War), the *Neutralizer*-Class Bomber has seen little use, as its primary purpose is strafing enemy [Weapon Emplacements](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Emplacements) and braking open defensible positions. However, with the rise in sedition by Roan Fel loyalists, the Empire has begun calling in *Neutralizer*-Class Bomber for air strikes on positions suspected of harboring sympathizers to the deposed Emperor.
