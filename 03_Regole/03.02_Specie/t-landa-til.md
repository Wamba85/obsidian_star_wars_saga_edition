*t'landa Til Species data created by Wikia user SunsetMage001.*

The t'landa Til are a massive, quadrupedal, sentient species distantly related to the [Hutts](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Hutts). Their most unique feature is the males' Exultation, a form of sonic vibration used to attract mates, which can create powerful euphoric effects in others.

### t'landa Til Characteristics
**Personality:** Considering their relationship to the Hutts, many t'landa Til are no less ruthless, using their natural mental abilities to confuse and dominate "lesser" [Species](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Species), posing as false priests or even gods. 

**Physical Description:** The t'landa Til have long, whipping tails and four trunk-like legs with huge, padded feet. They also have a tiny pair of arms with delicate hands. Their faces somewhat resemble [Hutts](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Hutts) save for a long, thick horn above their snout. Their skin is thick, oily, and leathery and hangs in creases, wrinkles, and loose folds. 

**[Age Groups](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Age_Groups):** t'landa Til age at the following stages:

| CHILD | YOUNG ADULT | ADULT | MIDDLE AGE | OLD | VENERABLE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1-70 Years | 71-90 Years | 91-450 Years | 451-649 Years | 650-749 Years | 750+ Years |

**Homeworld:** The t'landa Til originally shared their homeworld of [Varl](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Varl) with the [Hutts](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Hutts), prior to its destruction. They now exist in many colonies throughout Hutt Space, particularly the planet [Ylesia](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Ylesia).

**Languages:** t'landa Til speak their own language of t'landa Til, and learn Huttese during childhood.

**Example Names:** Korraj, Palazidar, Pohtarza, Tarrz, Teroenza, Tilenna, Veratil.

**Adventurers:** t'landa Til often make for competent [Nobles](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Nobles), due to their natural mental abilities. At higher levels, many become [Charlatans](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Charlatans). 

### t'landa Til Species Traits
t'landa Til share the following Species Traits:

- **Ability Modifiers:** Male t'landa Til receive a +2 bonus to their [Charisma](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Charisma), but suffer -2 penalties to both their [Dexterity](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Dexterity) and [Wisdom](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Wisdom). Female t'landa Til receive a +2 bonus to their [Wisdom](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Wisdom), but suffer a -2 penalty to their [Dexterity](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Dexterity).
- **Large Size:** As Large creatures, t'landa Til take a -1 size penalty to their [Reflex Defense](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reflex_Defense) and a -5 size penalty on [Stealth](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Stealth) checks, and they gain a +5 size bonus to their [Damage Threshold](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Threshold). Their lifting and carrying limits are double those of Medium characters.
- **Speed:** t'landa Til have a base speed of 6 squares.
- **Exultation (Male)**: By spending a [Full-Round Action](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Full-Round_Action) to produce deep vibrations from their throat, a male t'landa Til gains a +5 Species bonus to [Persuasion](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Persuasion) checks made to [Change Attitude](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Change_Attitude). This is a mind-affecting effect.
- **Force Resistance:** A t'landa Til's sharp mind allows them to resist Force abilities. t'landa Til receive a +5 bonus to their [Will Defense](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Will_Defense) against any use of the [Use the Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Use_the_Force) skill.
- **Mental Fortitude (Female):** Female t'landa Til have grown somewhat wise to the siren song of the males, and gain a +2 [Species](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Species) bonus to their [Will Defense](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Will_Defense) against [Deception](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Deception) and [Persuasion](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Persuasion) checks.
- **Natural Weapons:** A t'landa Til has Natural Weapons, in the form of its tough horn. When a t'landa Til makes an [Unarmed](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Unarmed) attack, it may choose to use its Natural Weapons, dealing 1d8 points of Piercing damage with the attack instead of normal [Unarmed](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Unarmed) damage. A t'landa Til is always considered armed with their Natural Weapons.
- **Stability:** A t'landa Til's four legs grant them a +5 stability bonus to resist attempts to knock them [Prone](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Prone).
- **Automatic Languages:** All t'landa Til can speak, read, and write Huttese and t'landa Til.
