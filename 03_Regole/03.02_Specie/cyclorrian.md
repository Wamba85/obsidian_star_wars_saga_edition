*Cyclorrian Species data created by Wikia user SunsetMage001.*

Cyclorrians are a technologically advanced Species of insectoid engineers that live in hives alongside the rest of their swarm. Their engineers at the Cyclor Shipyards are often at the forefront of Starship innovation and are responsible for many celebrated designs, both luxurious and highly practical.

Cyclorrian swarms usually form close communities that travel as a single unit, making lone Cyclorrians a rare sight.
### Cyclorrian Characteristics
**Personality:** Cyclorrians are a highly gregarious Species, particularly among their own kind.

**Physical Description:** Cyclorrians are a short, insectoid species with antennae, compound eyes and adhesive feet.

**[Age Groups](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Age_Groups):** Cyclorrians age at the following stages:

| CHILD | YOUNG ADULT | ADULT | MIDDLE AGE | OLD | VENERABLE |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1-5 Years | 6-8 Years | 9-32 Years | 33-46 Years | 47-59 Years | 60+ Years |

**Homeworld:** [Cyclor](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclor), a green-brown planet in [The Mid Rim](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Mid_Rim), known for its shipyards.

**Languages:** Cyclorrians speak their own language of Cyclorric as well as Basic.

**Example Names:** Charzvoz Rezvic, Izrimz Vazer, Hazziz, Vazzet Dipterz, Wizzich Mozzer, Zaret Ptezach.

**Adventurers:** Cyclorrians can be of any [Heroic Class](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Heroic_Class), but are most often [Technicians](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Technicians).
### Cyclorrian Species Traits
Cyclorrians share the following Species Traits:
- **Ability Modifiers:** All Cyclorrians receive a +2 bonus to their [Intelligence](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Intelligence), but suffer a -2 penalty to their [Strength](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Strength). Cyclorrians have a knack for engineering, but their short stature limits their strength.
- **Small Size:** As Small creatures, Cyclorrians gain a +1 size bonus to their [Reflex Defense](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reflex_Defense) and a +5 size bonus on [Stealth](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Stealth) checks. However, their lifting and carrying limits are three-quarters of those of Medium characters.
- **Speed:** Cyclorrians have a base speed of 4 squares.
- **Darkvision:** Cyclorrians ignore [Concealment](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Concealment) (including [Total Concealment](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Total_Concealment)) from darkness. However, they cannot perceive colors in total darkness.
- **Special Equipment:** Cyclorrians suffer limitations outside their native atmosphere. Without an Antiox Breath Mask, a Cyclorrian begins to [Suffocate](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Suffocate). A replacement mask costs 2,000 credits (500 on [Cyclor](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclor)), and a year's supply of filters costs 200 credits (50 on [Cyclor](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Cyclor)). Cyclorrian characters begin play with these items at no cost.
    - An Antiox Breath Mask is poisonous to other [Species](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Species).
- **Stability:** Cyclorrians possess adhesive feet that grant them a +5 stability bonus to resist attempts to knock them [Prone](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Prone).
- **Tinkerer:** A Cyclorrian can choose to reroll any [Mechanics](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Mechanics) check, but must keep the second result, even if it is worse. In addition, [Mechanics](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Mechanics) is always a Class Skill for Cyclorrians.
- **Automatic Languages:** All Cyclorrians can speak, read, and write both Basic and Cyclorric.
