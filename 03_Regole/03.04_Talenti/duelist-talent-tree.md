*Reference Book: Star Wars Saga Edition Core Rulebook*

Through rigorous training and experience, you become one of the greatest swordfighters in the galaxy.

#### **Force Fortification**
As a [Reaction](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reaction), you can spend a [Force Point](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Point) to negate a Critical Hit scored against you, and take normal damage instead. You can spend this [Force Point](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Point) even if you've already spent a [Force Point](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Point) earlier in the round.

#### **Greater Weapon Focus (Lightsabers)**
*Prerequisite:* [Weapon Focus (Lightsabers)](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Focus_(Lightsabers))

You gain a +1 bonus on melee attack rolls with [Lightsabers](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsabers). This bonus stacks with the bonus granted by the [Weapon Focus (Lightsabers)](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Focus_(Lightsabers)) feat.

#### **Greater Weapon Specialization (Lightsabers)**
*Prerequisites:* **[Weapon Specialization (Lightsabers)](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Specialization_(Lightsabers))**, **Greater Weapon Focus (Lightsabers)**

You gain a +2 bonus on melee damage rolls with [Lightsabers](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsabers). This bonus stacks with the bonus granted by the **[Weapon Specialization (Lightsabers)](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Specialization_(Lightsabers))** Talent.

#### **Multiattack Proficiency (Lightsabers)**
Whenever you make multiple attacks with any type of [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) as part of a [Full Attack](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Full_Attack), you reduce the penalty on your attack rolls by 2.

You can take this Talent multiple times; each time you take this Talent, you reduce the penalty on your attack rolls by an additional 2.

#### **Severing Strike**
*See also: [Maiming Foes](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Maiming_Foes)*

When you deal damage with a [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) that is equal to or greater than both the target's current Hit Points and the target's [Damage Threshold](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Threshold) (that is, when you would deal enough damage to kill your target), you may choose to use this Talent.

Instead of dealing full damage, you instead deal half damage to your target and move it -1 step on the [Condition Track](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Condition_Track). In addition, you sever one of your target's arms at the wrist or elbow joint, or one of the target's legs at the knee or ankle joint (your choice).

Severing part of an arm prevents the target from wielding weapons or using tools in that hand, and imposes a -5 penalty on [Skill Checks](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Skill_Checks) and [Ability Checks](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Checks) keyed to [Strength](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Strength) and [Dexterity](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Dexterity).

Severing part of a leg knocks the target [Prone](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Prone), reduces the target's [Speed](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Speed) by half, reduces its [Carrying Capacity](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Carrying_Capacity) by half, and imposes a -5 penalty on [Skill Checks](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Skill_Checks) and [Ability Checks](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Ability_Checks) keyed to [Strength](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Strength) and [Dexterity](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Dexterity).

Because of the severity of such an injury, losing part of a limb causes a [Persistent Condition](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Persistent_Condition) that can only be removed by having [Surgery](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Surgery) successfully performed on you. A [Cybernetic Prosthesis](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Cybernetic_Prosthesis) negates these reductions and penalties.

## Additional Duelist Talents

#### **Improved Lightsaber Throw**
*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Force Unleashed Campaign Guide](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Force_Unleashed_Campaign_Guide)*

*Prerequisite:* **[Lightsaber Throw](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber_Throw)**

You can spend a [Force Point](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Point) as a [Standard Action](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Standard_Action) to throw your [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) at a group of opponents. You make a single ranged attack roll (treating the [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) as a [Thrown Weapon](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Weapon)) and compare the result to the [Reflex Defense](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reflex_Defense) of all targets in a 6-square line originating in your square.

If your attack roll result exceeds a target's [Reflex Defense](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reflex_Defense), you deal normal [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) damage to that target (dealing half damage if you fail to exceed the target's [Reflex Defense](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reflex_Defense)). This attack is considered an [Area Attack](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Area_Attack).

You can pull your [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) back to your hand as a [Swift Action](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Swift_Action) by making a DC 20 [Use the Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Use_the_Force) check.

#### **Improved Riposte**
*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Knights_of_the_Old_Republic_Campaign_Guide)*

*Prerequisites:* **[Block](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Block)**, **[Riposte](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Riposte)**

When you successfully make a **[Riposte](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Riposte)** attack using the **[Riposte](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Riposte)** Talent, you do not count the **[Block](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Block)** Talent use that triggered the **[Riposte](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Riposte)** (thus, you take no cumulative penalty to [Use the Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Use_the_Force) checks from that **[Block](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Block)** attempt).

Subsequent **[Block](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Block)** attempts before the beginning of your next turn impose these penalties as normal.

#### **Improved Redirect**
*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Knights_of_the_Old_Republic_Campaign_Guide)*

*Prerequisites:* **[Deflect](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Deflect)**, **[Redirect Shot](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Redirect_Shot)**

Once per turn, when you successfully Redirect an attack using the **[Redirect Shot](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Redirect_Shot)** Talent, you do not count the **[Deflect](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Deflect)** Talent use that triggered the redirected attack (thus, you take no cumulative penalty to [Use the Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Use_the_Force) checks from that **[Deflect](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Deflect)** attempt).

Subsequent **[Deflect](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Deflect)** attempts before the beginning of your next turn impose these penalties as normal.

#### **Lightsaber Form Savant**
*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Jedi Academy Training Manual](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Jedi_Academy_Training_Manual)*

Once per encounter as a [Swift Action](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Swift_Action), you can return any one spent [Force Power](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Power) with the [*[Lightsaber Form](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber_Form)*] descriptor to your [Force Power Suite](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Power_Suite) without spending a [Force Point](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Point).

You can select this Talent multiple times. Each time you select it, you can use it one additional time per encounter.

#### **Thrown Lightsaber Mastery**
*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Force Unleashed Campaign Guide](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Force_Unleashed_Campaign_Guide)*

*Prerequisites:* **Improved Lightsaber Throw**, **[Lightsaber Throw](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber_Throw)**

Any target successfully struck by a [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) you [Throw](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Throw) moves at half [Speed](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Speed) (round down) until the beginning of your next turn.

#### **Shoto Master**
*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Jedi Academy Training Manual](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Jedi_Academy_Training_Manual)*

When you wield both a One-Handed [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) and a Light [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) (typically a [Shoto Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Shoto_Lightsaber) or a [Guard Shoto](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Guard_Shoto)), you can consider the One-Handed [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) to be a Light Weapon.

Additionally, if you have the **[Lightsaber Defense](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber_Defense)** Talent, you can activate the Talent as a [Free Action](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Free_Action) on your turn (instead of a [Swift Action](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Swift_Action)) whenever you wield both a One-Handed [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber) and a Light [Lightsaber](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Lightsaber).
