*Reference Book: [Star Wars Saga Edition Jedi  Academy Training Manual](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Star_Wars_Saga_Edition_Jedi__Academy_Training_Manual)*

[The Aing-Tii Monks](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Aing-Tii_Monks) have an understanding of [The Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Force) that is different from [The Jedi](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Jedi) and most other [Force Traditions](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Traditions). [The Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Force) has many more aspects than just a Light Side and a Dark Side, and it is most useful for facilitating freedom of travel.

You must be a member of [The Aing-Tii Monks](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/The_Aing-Tii_Monks) Force Tradition to select any of the Talents described below.

#### **Aura of Freedom**
All allies within 6 squares of you gain a +5 [Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force) bonus on [Skill Checks](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Skill_Checks) or [Grapple](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Grapple) checks made to escape [Grapples](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Grapples) or other [Immobilizing](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Immobilizing) [Hazards](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Hazards).

Additionally, whenever an ally within 6 squares of you is moved against its will (such as by the *[Move Object](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Move_Object)* Force Power or the [[03_Regole/03.05_Feat/bantha-rush.md|Bantha Rush]] feat), you can spend a [Force Point](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Point) as a [Reaction](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reaction) to negate the forced movement entirely.

#### **Folded Space Mastery**
*Prerequisite:* *[Fold Space](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Fold_Space)*

While you are the [Pilot](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Pilot_(Vehicle_Combat)) of a [Vehicle](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Vehicle), you can use the *[Fold Space](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Fold_Space)* Force Power to move the [Vehicle](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Vehicle) across long distances. If your [Use the Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Use_the_Force) check to activate the Force Power is sufficient to move an object the same size as your [Vehicle](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Vehicle) (or larger), you can use the Force Power to move your [Vehicle](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Vehicle) and all of its occupants safely to the desired destination.

You can use your [Use the Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Use_the_Force) check result instead of a [Use Computer](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Use_Computer) check, as though performing [Astrogation](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Astrogation). This otherwise uses the normal rules for Hyperspace travel, though travel is instantaneous and requires no [Hyperdrive](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Hyperdrive).

#### **Liberate**
*Prerequisite:* **Aura of Freedom**

You can spend a [Force Point](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Point) as a [Swift Action](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Swift_Action) to designate one ally within 12 squares of you and in your line of sight that is currently [Grabbed](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Grabbed), [Grappled](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Grappled), or [Immobilized](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Immobilized); that target automatically escapes from the [Grab](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Grab) or [Grapple](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Grapple) (or the [Immobilization](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Immobilization) effect is removed), and the target can move up to half its Speed immediately as a [Reaction](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reaction).

This movement does not provoke [Attacks of Opportunity](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Attacks_of_Opportunity).

#### **Many Shades of the Force**
*Prerequisite:* [[03_Regole/03.05_Feat/force-training.md|Force Training]]

Choose one [Force Power](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Power) with the [*[Dark Side](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Side)*] or [*[Light Side](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Light_Side)*] descriptor in your [Force Power Suite](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Power_Suite). That [Force Power](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Power) is no longer considered to have that descriptor for you.

#### **Spatial Integrity**
While you are aboard a [Vehicle](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Vehicle), you can spend a [Force Point](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Force_Point) as a [Reaction](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Reaction) to the [Vehicle](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Vehicle) taking damage; you make a [Use the Force](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Use_the_Force) check and reduce the damage the [Vehicle](https://swse.fandom.com/wiki/Vehicle) takes by the check result.

This reduction occurs after both DR and SR are applied to the damage.
